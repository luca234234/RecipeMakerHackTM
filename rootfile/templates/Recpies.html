Tomato, Mushroom, and Salami Salad with Apple Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • 8-10 mushrooms
  • 6-8 slices of salami
  • 1 apple

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes into wedges and place them in a large salad bowl.
  2. Clean the mushrooms and slice them. Add the sliced mushrooms to the bowl with tomatoes.
  3. Cut the salami slices into thin strips and add them to the bowl as well.
  4. Core the apple and cut it into small cubes. Add the apple cubes to the rest of the ingredients in the bowl.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together apple cider vinegar, olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  6. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  7. Let the salad marinate for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  8. Serve the tomato, mushroom, and salami salad with apple vinaigrette as a refreshing appetizer or as a side dish with your favorite main course.
